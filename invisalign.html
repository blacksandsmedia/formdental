Invisalign Clear Aligners

Straighten your teeth discreetly with the world's most advanced clear aligner system

The Clear Alternative to Braces

Invisalign is a revolutionary orthodontic treatment that uses a series of custom-made, virtually invisible aligners to gradually straighten your teeth. Perfect for adults and teens who want to improve their smile without the visibility and inconvenience of traditional metal braces.

What is Invisalign?

Invisalign uses advanced 3D imaging technology to create a series of custom-made clear aligners specifically for your teeth. Each aligner is worn for about two weeks before moving to the next in the series, gradually shifting your teeth into their ideal position.

How Invisalign Works

  • 3D digital scan of your teeth and bite
  • Custom treatment plan showing tooth movement
  • Series of clear aligners manufactured for your teeth
  • Wear each aligner for 20-22 hours per day
  • Change aligners every 1-2 weeks as directed
  • Regular progress check-ups every 6-8 weeks

Invisalign vs Traditional Braces

  • Virtually invisible: Clear aligners are hardly noticeable
  • Removable: Take them out to eat, drink, brush, and floss
  • Comfortable: No metal brackets or wires to irritate
  • Convenient: Fewer dental visits required
  • Predictable: See your results before treatment starts

Invisalign Treatment Options

Invisalign Comprehensive

Our most comprehensive treatment option for complex cases requiring significant tooth movement. Includes unlimited aligners and refinements to achieve optimal results.

Invisalign Lite

Ideal for minor to moderate tooth alignment issues. Uses up to 14 sets of aligners and is perfect for patients with previous orthodontic treatment who need minor adjustments.

Invisalign i7

Designed for very minor corrections using just 7 sets of aligners. Perfect for small spacing issues or minor relapse after previous orthodontic treatment.

Vivera Retainers

Custom-made retainers to maintain your new smile after Invisalign treatment. Made from the same advanced material as Invisalign aligners.

What Can Invisalign Treat?

Invisalign can effectively treat a wide range of orthodontic issues:

Common Issues Invisalign Addresses

  • Crowded teeth: When there's not enough space for teeth to align properly
  • Spacing issues: Gaps between teeth
  • Overbite: Upper teeth bite over the lower teeth
  • Underbite: Lower teeth protrude past upper teeth
  • Crossbite: Upper and lower jaws are misaligned
  • Open bite: Upper and lower teeth don't meet when biting

Complex Cases

Advanced Invisalign techniques can now treat more complex cases that previously required traditional braces, including:

  • Severe crowding
  • Significant bite issues
  • Rotated teeth
  • Extracted tooth cases

The Invisalign Process

Initial Consultation

We'll examine your teeth and discuss your smile goals to determine if Invisalign is right for you. We'll explain the process, timeline, and expected results.

3D Digital Planning

Using advanced iTero scanning technology, we create precise 3D images of your teeth. This allows us to plan your treatment and show you a virtual preview of your new smile.

Custom Aligner Creation

Your aligners are custom-manufactured using advanced technology. Each aligner is precisely designed to move specific teeth in the right direction at the right time.

Treatment Begins

You'll receive your first few sets of aligners along with detailed instructions. Most patients see noticeable improvements within the first few weeks.

Progress Monitoring

Regular check-ups every 6-8 weeks allow us to monitor your progress and provide you with new aligners. Treatment typically takes 6-18 months.

Retention Phase

After treatment, you'll receive Vivera retainers to maintain your new smile for years to come.

Who is a Good Candidate?

Invisalign is suitable for most people with orthodontic concerns, particularly:

Ideal Candidates

  • Adults and teenagers (14+ years)
  • Those with mild to moderate orthodontic issues
  • Patients who value discretion and convenience
  • People with active lifestyles
  • Professionals who need to maintain appearance
  • Those committed to wearing aligners 20-22 hours daily

Important Considerations

  • Must wear aligners consistently for best results
  • Requires good oral hygiene
  • Need to remove aligners for eating and drinking
  • Regular dental check-ups during treatment

Caring for Your Aligners

Daily Care Routine

  • Rinse aligners with cool water before and after wear
  • Brush aligners gently with a soft toothbrush
  • Use Invisalign cleaning crystals or gentle cleanser
  • Store aligners in their case when not wearing
  • Avoid hot water which can warp the plastic

Oral Hygiene During Treatment

  • Brush and floss after every meal
  • Clean teeth before putting aligners back in
  • Use fluoride toothpaste and mouthwash
  • Maintain regular dental cleanings

What to Avoid

  • Eating or drinking with aligners in (except water)
  • Smoking or chewing gum while wearing aligners
  • Using harsh chemicals to clean aligners
  • Exposing aligners to high temperatures

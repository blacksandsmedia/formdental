Professional Teeth Whitening

Achieve a brighter, more confident smile with our safe and effective whitening treatments

Brighten Your Smile

Professional teeth whitening is one of the most popular cosmetic dental treatments, offering dramatic results in a safe, controlled environment. Our whitening treatments can lighten your teeth by several shades, revealing a brighter, more youthful smile.

Why Choose Professional Whitening?

While there are many over-the-counter whitening products available, professional whitening offers superior results with greater safety and effectiveness.

Professional Advantages

  • Stronger, more effective whitening agents
  • Customised treatment for your specific needs
  • Professional supervision for safety
  • Faster, more dramatic results
  • Even whitening across all teeth
  • Reduced sensitivity with proper application
  • Long-lasting results with proper maintenance

Our Whitening Options

Power Whitening (In-Surgery)

Our in-surgery power whitening treatment delivers immediate, dramatic results in just one appointment. Using professional-strength whitening gel and advanced light technology, we can lighten your teeth by up to 8 shades in approximately 90 minutes.

Take-Home Whitening Kits

For those who prefer to whiten at home, we provide custom-made whitening trays with professional-grade whitening gel. This allows you to whiten your teeth at your own pace whilst achieving professional results.

Combination Treatment

Many patients choose to combine both treatments for optimal results - starting with power whitening for immediate impact, followed by take-home kits for maintenance.

The Whitening Process

Initial Consultation

We begin with a thorough examination to assess your teeth and gums, ensuring you're a good candidate for whitening. We'll discuss your goals and recommend the best treatment option for you.

Pre-treatment Preparation

Before whitening, we'll ensure your teeth are clean and healthy. Any necessary dental work should be completed first, as whitening only affects natural tooth enamel.

Whitening Treatment

During power whitening, we protect your gums and soft tissues before applying the whitening gel. For take-home kits, we create custom trays for comfortable, effective application.

Post-treatment Care

We'll provide detailed aftercare instructions to maximise your results and minimise any sensitivity. Regular follow-up appointments help maintain your bright smile.

What Causes Tooth Discolouration?

Understanding the causes of tooth staining helps us provide the most effective treatment:

Extrinsic Stains (Surface Stains)

  • Coffee, tea, and red wine
  • Tobacco use
  • Certain foods (berries, curry, tomato sauce)
  • Poor oral hygiene

Intrinsic Stains (Internal Stains)

  • Natural ageing process
  • Certain medications (tetracycline)
  • Excessive fluoride during tooth development
  • Trauma to teeth
  • Genetics

Who is a Good Candidate?

Most people can benefit from teeth whitening, but ideal candidates have:

  • Healthy teeth and gums
  • Realistic expectations about results
  • Yellow or brown tooth discolouration
  • No extensive dental restorations on front teeth
  • No significant tooth sensitivity

When Whitening May Not Be Suitable

  • Pregnant or breastfeeding women
  • Children under 16 years old
  • Severe tooth sensitivity
  • Extensive decay or gum disease
  • Unrealistic expectations

Maintaining Your Results

With proper care, your whitening results can last for years. Here's how to maintain your bright smile:

Immediately After Treatment (24-48 hours)

  • Avoid staining foods and drinks
  • Don't smoke or use tobacco products
  • Use a straw for beverages when possible
  • Brush gently with a soft-bristled toothbrush

Long-term Maintenance

  • Maintain excellent oral hygiene
  • Use whitening toothpaste occasionally
  • Regular dental cleanings every 6 months
  • Touch-up treatments as needed
  • Limit staining substances

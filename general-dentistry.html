General Dentistry

Complete oral health care in a relaxing environment, from routine cleanings to restorative treatments

Comprehensive Dental Care

General dentistry forms the foundation of good oral health. At FORM DENTAL, we provide comprehensive dental care services in a comfortable, modern environment. From routine checkups to complex restorative treatments, we're here to maintain and improve your oral health throughout your life.

What is General Dentistry?

General dentistry encompasses the prevention, diagnosis, and treatment of a wide variety of conditions, disorders, and diseases affecting the teeth, gums, and maxillofacial region of the body.

Our Comprehensive Approach

  • Preventive care to maintain oral health
  • Early detection and treatment of dental problems
  • Restorative treatments to repair damaged teeth
  • Education on proper oral hygiene techniques
  • Personalised treatment plans for each patient
  • Modern technology for accurate diagnosis

Why Choose FORM DENTAL for General Dentistry?

  • Experienced and qualified dental professionals
  • State-of-the-art equipment and techniques
  • Comfortable, spa-like environment
  • Comprehensive care under one roof
  • Flexible appointment scheduling
  • Focus on patient comfort and anxiety management

Routine Dental Care

Regular dental care is essential for maintaining optimal oral health and preventing serious dental problems.

Dental Examinations

Comprehensive dental examinations include:

  • Visual examination of teeth, gums, and oral tissues
  • Digital X-rays when necessary
  • Oral cancer screening
  • Bite analysis and jaw function assessment
  • Assessment of existing dental work
  • Discussion of any concerns or symptoms

Professional Dental Cleanings

Our thorough cleanings include:

  • Removal of plaque and tartar buildup
  • Professional polishing of teeth
  • Fluoride treatment for cavity prevention
  • Gum health assessment
  • Oral hygiene instruction and advice

Digital X-rays

Our digital X-ray technology offers:

  • Reduced radiation exposure
  • Instant image availability
  • Enhanced image quality for accurate diagnosis
  • Environmental benefits (no chemical processing)
  • Easy storage and sharing capabilities

Restorative Dentistry

When dental problems occur, we provide a full range of restorative treatments to restore function and aesthetics.

White Fillings (Composite Restorations)

Our tooth-coloured fillings offer:

  • Natural appearance that matches your teeth
  • Mercury-free composition
  • Strong bond to tooth structure
  • Conservative treatment preserving healthy tooth
  • Suitable for front and back teeth

Dental Crowns

Crowns are recommended when:

  • Large portions of tooth are damaged or decayed
  • Root canal treatment has been performed
  • Teeth are severely worn or cracked
  • Aesthetic improvement is desired
  • Dental implants need restoration

Root Canal Treatment

When the nerve inside a tooth becomes infected or damaged, root canal treatment can save the tooth:

  • Removes infected or damaged nerve tissue
  • Eliminates pain and infection
  • Preserves the natural tooth
  • Prevents extraction and replacement
  • Modern techniques ensure comfort

Dental Extractions

When tooth removal is necessary, we ensure:

  • Gentle, minimally traumatic extraction techniques
  • Comprehensive aftercare instructions
  • Options for tooth replacement
  • Management of post-extraction healing

Emergency Dental Care

Dental emergencies can occur at any time. We provide prompt care for urgent dental problems.

Common Dental Emergencies

  • Severe tooth pain or dental abscess
  • Broken, chipped, or knocked-out teeth
  • Lost fillings or crowns
  • Soft tissue injuries
  • Swelling or facial trauma
  • Orthodontic emergencies

What to Do in a Dental Emergency

  • Contact our practice immediately
  • Apply cold compress for swelling
  • Save any broken tooth pieces
  • Take over-the-counter pain relief if needed
  • Avoid very hot or cold foods

Emergency Appointment Availability

We understand that dental emergencies can't wait. We reserve time in our schedule for urgent cases and will see emergency patients as quickly as possible.

Oral Health Education

We believe that patient education is crucial for maintaining excellent oral health between visits.

Proper Brushing Technique

  • Use a soft-bristled toothbrush
  • Brush for at least two minutes
  • Use fluoride toothpaste
  • Brush at 45-degree angle to gumline
  • Replace toothbrush every 3-4 months

Flossing and Interdental Care

  • Floss daily to remove plaque between teeth
  • Use proper flossing technique
  • Consider interdental brushes for larger spaces
  • Water flossers can supplement traditional flossing

Diet and Oral Health

  • Limit sugary and acidic foods and drinks
  • Choose tooth-friendly snacks
  • Drink plenty of water
  • Avoid frequent snacking
  • Consider the timing of meals and drinks

Maintaining Your Oral Health

Good oral health is a partnership between you and your dental team.

Regular Visit Schedule

  • Checkups every 6 months for most patients
  • More frequent visits for high-risk patients
  • Hygienist appointments as recommended
  • Prompt attention to any problems

At-Home Care

  • Brush twice daily with fluoride toothpaste
  • Floss daily
  • Use antimicrobial mouthwash if recommended
  • Maintain a healthy diet
  • Avoid tobacco products
  • Wear protective mouthguards for sports

Warning Signs to Watch For

  • Persistent tooth pain
  • Sensitivity to hot or cold
  • Bleeding or swollen gums
  • Bad breath that won't go away
  • Loose teeth
  • Changes in bite or jaw alignment

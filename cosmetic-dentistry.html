Cosmetic Dentistry

Transform your smile with our advanced aesthetic dental treatments

Enhance Your Natural Beauty

At FORM DENTAL, our cosmetic dentistry treatments are designed to enhance your natural smile whilst ensuring optimal oral health. We combine advanced techniques with an artistic eye to create beautiful, natural-looking results that boost your confidence.

What is Cosmetic Dentistry?

Cosmetic dentistry focuses on improving the appearance of your teeth, gums, and overall smile. Unlike general dentistry which addresses oral health issues, cosmetic dentistry is primarily concerned with dental aesthetics, though many treatments also provide restorative benefits.

Our Approach

We believe that the best cosmetic dentistry looks completely natural. Our treatments are carefully planned to:

  • Enhance your unique facial features
  • Maintain optimal oral health
  • Create results that look and feel natural
  • Boost your confidence and self-esteem
  • Provide long-lasting, beautiful results

Cosmetic Treatments We Offer

Composite Bonding & Veneers

Transform your smile with natural-looking composite resin that can reshape, repair, and enhance your teeth. Our composite bonding techniques include edge bonding and full composite veneers.

Professional Teeth Whitening

Achieve a brighter, more radiant smile with our professional whitening treatments, available as both in-surgery power whitening and take-home kits.

Smile Makeovers

Comprehensive smile transformations that may combine multiple treatments to achieve your perfect smile. We create a personalised treatment plan based on your goals and facial features.

Aesthetic Restorations

White fillings and crowns that not only restore damaged teeth but also enhance the natural beauty of your smile.

The Treatment Process

Initial Consultation

During your comprehensive consultation, we'll discuss your smile goals, examine your teeth and gums, and explain the treatment options available to you. We may take photographs and impressions to help plan your treatment.

Treatment Planning

We'll create a personalised treatment plan that outlines the recommended procedures, timeline, and expected results. This ensures you're fully informed before beginning any treatment.

Treatment Delivery

Our treatments are performed with precision and care in our comfortable, modern practice. We use the latest techniques and materials to ensure excellent results.

Aftercare & Maintenance

We'll provide detailed aftercare instructions and schedule follow-up appointments to ensure your results are maintained long-term.

Benefits of Cosmetic Dentistry

  • Enhanced Confidence: A beautiful smile can significantly boost your self-esteem and confidence in social and professional situations
  • Natural Results: Modern cosmetic dentistry techniques create results that look completely natural
  • Improved Function: Many cosmetic treatments also improve the function of your teeth
  • Long-lasting: With proper care, cosmetic dental work can last many years
  • Minimally Invasive: Many modern techniques preserve your natural tooth structure
  • Customised Solutions: Treatments are tailored to your unique facial features and preferences

Who is a Good Candidate?

Cosmetic dentistry may be right for you if you have:

  • Stained or discoloured teeth
  • Chipped, cracked, or worn teeth
  • Gaps between teeth
  • Misshapen or uneven teeth
  • An uneven or gummy smile
  • Generally healthy teeth and gums

The best candidates have realistic expectations and are committed to maintaining good oral hygiene to preserve their results.

Patient Information

Everything you need to know about visiting FORM DENTAL

Welcome to FORM DENTAL

We want to ensure your visit is comfortable and stress-free. Here's everything you need to know about your dental care experience with us.

Your First Visit

Your initial consultation is designed to understand your dental health needs and goals. We'll take time to discuss your medical history, current concerns, and treatment preferences.

What to Bring

  • Valid photo identification
  • Recent X-rays (if available)
  • List of current medications
  • Previous dental treatment records
  • Insurance information (if applicable)

What to Expect

  • Comprehensive oral examination
  • Discussion of treatment options
  • Personalised treatment plan
  • Questions and concerns addressed
  • Follow-up appointment scheduling

Before Your Appointment

Preparation Tips

  • Arrive 15 minutes early for check-in
  • Brush and floss normally before your visit
  • Avoid eating immediately before treatment
  • Wear comfortable clothing
  • Bring headphones if you'd like to listen to music

If You're Anxious

We understand dental anxiety is common. Please let us know about any concerns so we can make your visit as comfortable as possible. We offer various relaxation techniques and will work at your pace.

After Your Treatment

Post-Treatment Care

  • Follow all post-treatment instructions
  • Take prescribed medications as directed
  • Attend all follow-up appointments
  • Contact us if you have concerns
  • Maintain good oral hygiene

Emergency Contact

If you experience a dental emergency outside of practice hours, please contact us immediately. We provide guidance and emergency care when needed.

Practice Policies

Appointment Scheduling

We recommend booking appointments well in advance. Please provide 24 hours' notice for any cancellations to avoid cancellation fees.

Payment Options

We accept cash, cheque, and card payments. Payment plans may be available for comprehensive treatments. Please discuss payment options during your consultation.

Hygiene and Safety

We maintain the highest standards of infection control and sterilisation. All equipment is properly cleaned and sterilised between patients for your safety.
